IXP-137 CORE

Autonomous, crash-safe experimental engine for cycle-driven field analysis.

The IXP-137 CORE ties together macro-cycles, micro-cycles, harmonic envelopes and directional stress-fields into a single, autonomous research engine. It does not predict reality. It lets you study how a universe of signals would behave if it tried.

Critical notice:
This system is strictly experimental and non-operational. It must not be used for real-world early warning, safety decisions, evacuation planning, or any hazard communication. It is a research instrument only.
